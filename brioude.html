🐮 Brioude à Saint-Flour 
The ligne de Brioude à Saint-Flour is a former railroad line in the Auvergne which opened in 1908 and closed in 1954. Over five days I hiked the entire 45-kilometer route, including a very scary walk through one of the tunnels!

Thanks to Vito for helping me with the one-way hikes by dropping me off and picking me up so many times!
